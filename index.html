SIDBI

• SIDBI has identified five centres viz. Pune, Chennai, New Delhi, Lucknow And Guwahati where existing nodal officers of MUDRA / STANDUP INDIA shall be advised to respond to finance related queries raised by the Women Entrepreneurs in addition to their existing responsibilities.

• SIDBI, in discussion with NITI Aayog, will extend support for setting up a call-centre support (inbound only) with one / two dedicated resources & a toll-free number through dedicated / trained agents of the identified call centre.

• SIDBI will also extend support by advising its me4WE volunteer pool to be engaged in the initiative.

• The identified nodal officers / agents shall be trained through NITI Aayog support

CRISIL Limited

• CRISIL with its rich experience, in grading of Micro-Finance institutions, and in rating/assessments of MSMEs will be an Enablement Partner of Niti Ayog, in providing technology enabled solutions for a basic verification of the credentials, and also in conducting a credit evaluation to enable credit access to the enterprises of women entrepreneurs

• These online offerings from CRISIL, an S&P Global company, known for its unbiased and independent assessments will help women entrepreneurs in establishing their credentials and thereby enhance the confidence of potential customers, suppliers, and lenders

Mann Deshi Foundation

Facebook India

Gyaan Shakti: #SheMeansBusiness Program - Training of Women Entrepreneurs on Social Media Marketing: Facebook is committed to train 20,000 women entrepreneurs across India in 2018 on social media marketing skills that they can use to build a new business online or grow their existing businesses. Since launching the #SheMeansBusiness program two years ago, we have so far trained more than 25,000 women entrepreneurs and have seen firsthand the impact.

• Karma Shakti: #SheLeadsTech Program – mentorship and support for women led tech startups: Facebook is committed to grow our #SheLeadsTech program from 100 women tech entrepreneurs to 350+ women tech entrepreneurs by end of 2018. The SheLeadsTech program from Facebook has been created to support women founded start-ups, with access to community, tools, mentorship and resources to overcome barriers and succeed in building a business in technology.

• Karma Shakti: Facebook Ad Credits: Facebook will provide ad credits to 100 women entrepreneurs that are selected through WEC to help them grow their businesses. The amount of ad credits will be decided in consultation with NITI Aayog and partners of WEC.

NASSCOM

1. Support on Work Spaces

NASSCOM through Startup warehouses in 10 states would make available space for 15 Women Entrepreneurs led StartUp across the locations on a nominal charges basis. Women employees/ Entrepreneurs are offered 50% discount on the base fees of Rs.4000 per month per seat. . In return, the StartUp warehouses provides for top class infrastructure including WIFI bandwidth, access to meeting rooms, conference rooms, cohorts, etc.

We would also work with our technology partners / and other business/legal/ financial support partners to make available their Start up Kits to the selected start up as is made available to NASSCOM 10000 StartUp program. The estimated benefits available are approximately USD 10,000 per start up per year as cloud credits from our partners

2. Women Tech Ship Program

Through this program and along with its partner, NASSCOM would conduct specific acceleration program as below for the women entrepreneurs to understand and execute:

• Business plan feasibility, Market analysis, Segmentation and Market Research guidance

• Company formation and regulatory compliance guidance

• Monthly Business Series-Training to develop business skills – skills to start and scale up

• Help with e-business and other aspects of ICT

ICAI

• ICAI would provide the panel of Chartered Accountants across the country who have consented to volunteer their services for the Start Up Initiative undertaken by GoI at minimum reasonable fee commensurate with the work and time involved. The said panel contains the list of 609 Members.

• ICAI would provide 2 experts per State wherein the scope of work would be provided by NITI Aayog.

• ICAI can arrange for venues free of cost for conducting different workshops by NITI Aayog. In this regard, the list of 80 plus auditorium of our Regional Councils as well as Branches all across the Country would be provided.

• Faculty support /Resource persons would be provided by ICAI to take sessions during the said workshops.

Digital Leadership Institute

1. Digital Leadership Institute will provide free training to 1000 women (Coming through the WEC platform) on areas of skill development like and beauty and wellness, fashion, health, accounting and taxation, competitive strategy, patent and company registration, PR and communication as well as digital marketing courses.

2. Digital Leadership Institute will collaborate with partners across different cities in India and provide offline training and workshops. Our goal is to conduct at-least 12 workshops (1 offline workshop per month) across different cities in India.

4. Digital Leadership Institute will create a separate landing page to capture the skill development interest and aspiration of the women coming in through the WEC platform to further enhance the curriculum
